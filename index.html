FAQ

How does D's Band work?
The D's Band is an application for transforming music files (more precisely MP4 files) through a decoding and encoding process that allows you to substitute, alterate or eliminate a channel just as if you were in production.
For what devices will the application be available on?
IPhone, IPad, Android and Windows Phone based devices.
For what operating systems will the app be available on?
Windows, Ubuntu and MacOsX.
Will you be providing musical content?
There is an area on the website were you can download content provided by users..
